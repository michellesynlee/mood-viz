my 2026 mood tracker

i have been tracking my mood every day since the start of 2026. i realized that there were patterns in my mood shifts, and i wanted to visualize them in a way that was more fun than just words on a notebook.

i have been adding a visualization every month, so check back at the end of each month to see the new visualizations!

january

i found january to quite literally have the ups and downs of waves, which is what inspired this visualization. like clockwork, every 2-4 days i found my mood shifting from a high to a low, and then back up again. hover over the dots on the waves to see more detail...
